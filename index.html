🔥 质押挖矿 / Staking

当前阶段（Pre-Staking）：
目前处于预质押签到奖励阶段。用户通过网页签到累积的 PAWLY，将在正式上市后由官方钱包统一空投发放。

正式 Staking 功能：
当 PAWLY 用户数量超过 1 万时，我们将正式开启链上质押挖矿功能。
Current Phase (Pre-Staking)：
We are currently in the pre-staking check-in reward phase. PAWLY accumulated through web check-ins will be airdropped after official launch.

Official Staking Function：
When PAWLY reaches 10,000 users, we will officially launch on-chain staking.

🐶 宠物支付 / Pet Payment

未来愿景：
PAWLY 将逐步发展成为宠物爱好者的日常支付工具。未来可在合作宠物店、兽医诊所、线上宠物商城直接使用 PAWLY 支付。
Future Vision：
PAWLY aims to become a daily payment tool for pet lovers. Users will be able to use PAWLY directly at partner pet stores and clinics.

❤️ 宠物慈善基金 / Pet Charity Fund

我们的承诺：
PAWLY 将把部分交易费用用于宠物慈善。每季度公开捐款给马来西亚及全球的动物收容所，社区可参与投票决定捐款方向。
Our Commitment：
PAWLY will allocate part of transaction fees to pet charity. Quarterly public donations to animal shelters in Malaysia and worldwide. Community can vote on donation directions.

🌟 关于 PAWLY / About PAWLY

PAWLY 的诞生，源于创造者这些年看到越来越多的人用心爱护动物。我们希望从一个可爱的宠物 meme 币，逐步发展成有实际价值的宠物生态系统。
PAWLY was born from seeing more people caring for animals. We hope to evolve from a cute pet meme coin into a real-value pet ecosystem.
