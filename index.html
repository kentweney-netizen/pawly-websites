🌟 关于 PAWLY / About PAWLY

中文：
PAWLY 的诞生，源于创造者这些年看到越来越多的人用心爱护动物。我们希望从一个可爱的宠物 meme 币，逐步发展成有实际价值的宠物生态系统。我一直是个爱宠之人。每天看着自己心爱的宠物在家中撒娇、陪伴，那种纯真而无条件的信任，让我第一次真正感受到动物带给人类的温暖与治愈。 渐渐地，这种爱不再只停留在自己的宠物身上。我开始关注那些被遗弃的流浪动物、被关在笼子里的表演动物，以及那些在野外濒临灭绝的珍稀物种。我突然意识到：如果连我们这些爱宠的人都不站出来，谁来为它们发声？ 于是，我决定用区块链和加密货币的力量，创造 PAWLY。我希望 PAWLY 不只是一枚可爱的 meme 币，而是能成为连接「爱宠」与「护生」的桥梁。未来，我希望 PAWLY 能进入国际动物保护组织，为濒危动物发声，让更多人明白——保护动物，不只是保护家中的爱宠，更是保护这个星球上所有值得被守护的生命。
English：
PAWLY was born from seeing more people caring for animals. We hope to evolve from a cute pet meme coin into a real-value pet ecosystem.I have always been a pet lover. Watching my own beloved pet act spoiled and accompany me every day, that pure and unconditional trust made me truly feel the warmth and healing that animals bring to humans for the first time. Gradually, this love never stayed only with my own pet. I began to care about abandoned stray animals, performing animals kept in cages, and rare species on the verge of extinction in the wild. I suddenly realized: if even those of us who love pets don’t speak up for them, who will? That’s why I decided to use the power of blockchain and cryptocurrency to create PAWLY. I hope PAWLY is not just a cute meme coin, but a bridge connecting “pet lovers” and “animal protectors.” In the future, I hope PAWLY can enter international animal protection organizations and speak up for endangered animals — proving that protecting animals is not only about the pets in our homes, but about all lives on this planet that deserve to be protected.

为什么动物保护如此重要？ / Why is Animal Protection So Important?

中文：
每年有数百万只动物被遗弃、虐待或面临灭绝。保护动物不仅是对生命的尊重，更是对地球生态平衡的守护。每一只被救助的动物，都是对人性的一次温暖呼唤。爱护动物，就是爱护我们共同的家园。
English：
Millions of animals are abandoned, abused, or facing extinction every year. Protecting animals is not only respecting life, but also safeguarding the ecological balance of our planet. Every rescued animal is a warm call to humanity. Caring for animals means caring for our shared home.

全球主要动物保护组织 / Major World Animal Protection Organizations

World Animal Protection（世界动物保护协会）
全球领先的动物保护组织，致力于消除动物苦难，推动动物福利立法和国际合作。
A global leading animal protection organization dedicated to ending animal suffering and promoting animal welfare legislation and international cooperation.
Humane Society International（国际人道协会）
全球最大动物保护组织之一，长期救助流浪动物、反对动物试验与虐待，推动动物福利政策。
One of the world’s largest animal protection organizations, long-term rescue of stray animals, opposition to animal testing and abuse, and promotion of animal welfare policies.
WWF（世界自然基金会）
保护濒危物种与栖息地，推动全球生物多样性保护和气候 action。
Protects endangered species and habitats, promotes global biodiversity conservation and climate action.
International Fund for Animal Welfare (IFAW)
专注于野生动物救援与栖息地保护的国际组织，长期从事动物救援与政策倡导。
An international organization focused on wildlife rescue and habitat protection, long-term engaged in animal rescue and policy advocacy.
