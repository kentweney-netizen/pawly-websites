🔥 质押挖矿 / Staking

当前阶段（Pre-Staking）：
目前处于预质押签到奖励阶段。用户通过网页签到累积的 PAWLY，将在正式上市后由官方钱包统一空投发放。

正式 Staking 功能：
当 PAWLY 用户数量超过 1 万时，我们将正式开启链上质押挖矿功能。持有者可将 PAWLY 质押，获得持续奖励。
Current Phase (Pre-Staking)：
We are currently in the pre-staking check-in reward phase. PAWLY accumulated through web check-ins will be airdropped from the official wallet after official launch.

Official Staking Function：
When the number of PAWLY users exceeds 10,000, we will officially launch on-chain staking. Holders can stake their PAWLY to earn ongoing rewards.

🐶 宠物支付 / Pet Payment

未来愿景：
PAWLY 将逐步发展成为宠物爱好者的日常支付工具。未来用户可以在合作宠物店、兽医诊所、线上宠物商城等场景，直接使用 PAWLY 支付宠物粮、玩具、医疗服务等费用，让爱宠生活更加便捷。
Future Vision：
PAWLY aims to become a daily payment tool for pet lovers. In the future, users will be able to use PAWLY directly at partner pet stores, veterinary clinics, and online pet shops to pay for pet food, toys, medical services, and more.

❤️ 宠物慈善基金 / Pet Charity Fund

我们的承诺：
PAWLY 将把部分交易费用和代币分配用于宠物慈善事业。每季度我们会公开捐款给马来西亚及全球的动物收容所和流浪动物救援组织。社区可参与投票决定捐款方向，真正实现透明、公开的公益。
Our Commitment：
PAWLY will allocate part of transaction fees and tokens to pet charity. Every quarter, we will make public donations to animal shelters and rescue organizations in Malaysia and worldwide. The community can vote on donation directions to achieve truly transparent philanthropy.

🌟 关于 PAWLY / About PAWLY

PAWLY 的诞生，源于创造者这些年看到越来越多的人用心爱护动物。我们希望从一个可爱的宠物 meme 币，逐步发展成有实际价值的宠物生态系统 —— 结合签到挖矿、宠物支付、慈善公益，让每一位爱宠人士都能参与并从中受益。
PAWLY was born from the creator's years of seeing more and more people caring for animals with love. We hope to evolve from a cute pet meme coin into an ecosystem with real value — combining check-in mining, pet payments, and charity, so every pet lover can participate and benefit.
