Privacy Policy

我们重视您的隐私 / We Value Your Privacy

PAWLY PETS（以下简称“我们”）重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。

PAWLY PETS (“we”, “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

我们收集的信息 / Information We Collect

我们收集的信息包括：邮箱地址（用于登录和通知）、Solana 钱包地址（用于绑定和交易）、签到记录、连续签到天数、PAWLY Points，以及设备信息和使用数据。

The information we collect includes: email address (for login and notifications), Solana wallet address (for binding and transactions), check-in records, streak days, PAWLY Points, device information, and usage data.

信息使用方式 / How We Use Your Information

我们使用您的信息来提供签到、积分兑换和钱包相关功能，计算和显示您的 PAWLY Points 与总 PAWLY 数量，并发送重要通知，以改善产品和服务。

We use your information to provide check-in, points redemption, and wallet-related features, to calculate and display your PAWLY Points and total PAWLY, and to send important notifications in order to improve our products and services.

钱包地址与区块链数据 / Wallet Address & Blockchain Data

我们不会存储您的私钥。钱包地址属于公开信息，链上交易记录可能被公开查看。我们仅使用钱包地址来识别用户身份和提供服务。

We do not store your private keys. Wallet addresses are public information, and on-chain transactions may be publicly viewable. We only use wallet addresses to identify users and provide services.

数据共享 / Data Sharing

我们不会出售您的个人信息。除非法律要求，否则不会与第三方共享您的个人数据。

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law.

数据安全 / Data Security

我们采取合理的安全措施保护您的数据。但请注意，区块链上的数据具有公开性质。

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, please note that data on the blockchain is public by nature.

您的权利 / Your Rights

您可以随时解绑钱包、删除账户，或联系我们要求访问、更正或删除您的个人信息。

You may unbind your wallet, delete your account, or contact us to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.
