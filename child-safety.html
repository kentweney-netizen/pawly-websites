PAWLY PETS - Child Safety Standards

PAWLY PETS - 儿童安全标准

English

PAWLY PETS is committed to protecting all users, especially minors, from child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

  • Our app does not allow users under 18 years old. Target audience is 18+.
  • We do not provide chat, social networking, or user-generated content features that enable CSAE.
  • Any report of child safety issues will be immediately reviewed and reported to relevant authorities if necessary.
  • Users can report child safety concerns through the in-app support or by emailing: kentkent5161@gmail.com
  • We fully comply with all applicable child protection laws and Google Play Child Safety Standards.

中文

PAWLY PETS 致力于保护所有用户，特别是未成年人，免受儿童性虐待和性剥削（CSAE）的侵害。

  • 本应用不允许 18 岁以下用户使用，目标受众为 18 岁以上。
  • 应用不提供聊天、社交网络或用户生成内容功能，无法用于 CSAE 行为。
  • 任何儿童安全相关举报将立即审查，必要时报告给相关机构。
  • 用户可通过应用内支持或发送邮件至 kentkent5161@gmail.com 举报儿童安全问题。
  • 我们完全遵守所有适用的儿童保护法律和 Google Play 儿童安全标准。
